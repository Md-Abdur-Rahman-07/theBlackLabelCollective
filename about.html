Our Story
The Black Label Collective — Built From Experience

Black Label Collective wasn’t built in an office — it started behind the wheel. Before anything else, I was the one picking people up, running routes, and making sure every ride actually felt like an experience. That’s where I learned what matters… and more importantly, what most companies get wrong.

I come from a background where you figure things out and invest in yourself. My father always lived by a simple principle — pay for something once, learn it, master it, and keep leveling up by putting yourself around people who are better than you. I took that blueprint and expanded on it.

This business wasn’t built with outside funding, shortcuts, or a safety net. No investors. No handouts. Just consistency, long hours, and a decision early on that there was no Plan B — so Plan A had to work.

After nearly 9 years in the industry, one thing became clear: the market was missing personality, flexibility, and real attention to the client experience. Too many services felt rigid, transactional, and disconnected.

So I built something different.

From private nights out to weddings, corporate events, and large-scale experiences across Austin, everything we do is designed around how people actually move — not strict routes, not limitations.

Every experience we run is built to feel effortless from start to finish. Because at this level, it’s not just about getting there — it’s about how you arrive.


— Jahleel Laurent
Founder, The Black Label Collective
